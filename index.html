Hi, I am Irene!


I am a designer, resaercher, and technologist with over seven year's experience in human-centered design and technology. I use both qualitative and quantitative user research methods, my expertise in design and development, and empathizing with users to help create products and services that bring values to people’s lives. I am currently an assistant professor in the Department of Computing and Software at McMaster University. Prior to joining Mac, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Connections Lab in School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University, where I am working on designing multimodal technologies for remote intergenerational collaborative activities. I completed my PhD in HCI at University of Minnesota, advised by Dr. Lana Yarosh.
